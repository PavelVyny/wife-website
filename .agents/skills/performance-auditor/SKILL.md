# Skill: Performance Auditor

Ensures the final site meets the highest speed and quality standards.

## Directives
- **Lighthouse Score:** Aim for 100 in Performance, Accessibility, and Best Practices.
- **Image Check:** verify `src`, `alt`, and explicit `width`/`height` to prevent CLS.
- **Bundle Analysis:** Minimized unused CSS/JS.
- **Vercel Best Practices:** Ensure correct headers and static optimization.

## Methodology
1. **Analyze Assets:** Check for unoptimized images or heavy fonts.
2. **Audit Components:** Review for redundant re-renders or heavy client-side JS.
3. **Pre-deployment Check:** Verify metabolic tags and social share images (OG).
